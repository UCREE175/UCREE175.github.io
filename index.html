My Journey of understanding the process of developing a Project.

The Low Noise Preamplifier is used to produce an amplified version of a weak signal gathered from a detector. In our case the detector is a hall sensor input. The purpose of this device is to mount it on top of an Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV).

I used Bluetooth and RFCOMM communication do send ASCII bits of data ,4 at a time. Converted them and displayed them as mV on the application.As well as making the application handle multiple threads.

This will show only my input in the group project. Everything here is what I personally did in the project.
